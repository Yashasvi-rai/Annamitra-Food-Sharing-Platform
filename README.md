# 🍱 Annamitra

> Connecting surplus food with those who need it, one meal at a time.

## 📖 Overview

Annamitra is a web-based food sharing platform designed to minimize food wastage by connecting food donors with individuals, NGOs, and communities in need. The platform enables users to donate surplus food, request food assistance, and contribute towards building a more sustainable and hunger-free society.

## ✨ Key Features

- 🍽️ Food Donation Management
- 🤝 Donor and Receiver Registration
- 📍 Easy Food Request System
- 🔔 Real-Time Request Tracking
- 📊 Organized Food Listings
- 📱 Responsive User Interface
- ♻️ Promotes Sustainable Food Distribution

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Millions of meals are wasted daily while many people struggle with food insecurity. Annamitra addresses this gap by creating a digital bridge between food donors and recipients, ensuring that edible surplus food reaches those who need it instead of ending up as waste.

## 🏗️ System Workflow

1. Donor registers and logs in.
2. Donor uploads available food details.
3. Food requests are generated by receivers.
4. Matching and allocation of food resources.
5. Successful distribution and tracking.
